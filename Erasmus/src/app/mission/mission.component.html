Our Mission

Vision

By 2025, ESN aims to become the global network of the Erasmus Generation, dedicated to enhancing international education and providing self-development opportunities to two million young people. We strive to foster intercultural understanding and create positive change in society.

Mission

Our mission is to enrich society through the presence of international students.

Values

  • Unity in diversity: We celebrate our differences while working towards a common aim and goal.
  • Students helping students: We passionately volunteer for the benefit of others.
  • Fun in friendship and respect: We enjoy relationships built on respect.
  • International dimension of life: We are open-minded, mobile, and eager to discover, cooperate, and interact to break borders.
  • Love for Europe as an area of peace and cultural exchange: We embrace and appreciate Europe's cultural richness.
  • Openness with tolerance: We understand and accept others, learning from their perspectives.
  • Cooperation in integration: We take a holistic approach to internationality and promote cooperation.
